Our Journey

From startup to infrastructure

Four years of relentless execution, one milestone at a time.

2022

Stackly founded in Salem, Tamil Nadu

A team of four power-systems engineers set out to solve India's EV charging reliability gap, incorporating under the Stackly brand with seed funding from climate-tech investors.

2022

First 10 commercial stations commissioned

Pilot installations across Salem and Coimbatore commercial zones proved a sub-24-hour deployment model. Uptime in the first 6 months: 99.4%.

2023

Stackly Cloud platform v1 launched

Real-time OCPP 2.0.1 management dashboard released — live energy telemetry, RFID access tiers, and automated fault reporting across all connected stations.

2024

Series-A & national expansion to 50 cities

₹42 Cr Series-A closed with a leading EV infrastructure fund. Operations scaled to 50 cities including Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune, and Ahmedabad.

2025

V2G bi-directional charging certified

Stackly became one of the first Indian EV infrastructure providers to achieve BIS certification for Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) capable hardware, enabling energy recovery and grid stabilisation.

2026

500-station milestone & Enterprise Zero-CAPEX launch

Today Stackly manages 500+ active stations serving 8,000+ businesses. Our Enterprise plan offers zero upfront hardware cost — Stackly owns, operates, and maintains; clients pay only per kWh dispensed.

What We Stand For

Principles that drive everything

Six values embedded in every product decision, every site survey, every support call.

Reliability First

A dead charger is a broken promise. Every component is rated for 200,000+ cycles and backed by a 4-hour on-site SLA.

Open Standards

We build on OCPP, OCPI, and ISO 15118 — no proprietary lock-in, interoperable with any compliant EV or backend system.

Climate Impact

Every kWh we dispense offsets petrol. We report carbon displacement for every station and publish annual sustainability accounts.

Data Transparency

Operators own their data. Real-time usage dashboards, export-ready CSV billing, and API access are included on every plan.

Honest Support

Our support team speaks plainly, owns problems, and tracks every ticket to resolution. No scripted deflections, ever.

Scalable by Design

From a single office charger to a 100-point highway corridor, Stackly's hardware and cloud platform scale without architectural changes.
