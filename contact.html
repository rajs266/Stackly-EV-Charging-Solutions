FAQ

Common questions answered

Can't find your answer here? Call us at +91 98765 43210 or email hello@stackly.in

How long does a commercial installation take?

For most commercial sites, Stackly completes installation within 2–5 working days of survey approval. Multi-bay hubs may take 5–10 days depending on civil requirements.

Is there really zero upfront hardware cost?

Yes — on our Enterprise plan Stackly retains ownership of the hardware. You pay only per kWh dispensed, with no upfront CAPEX. Maintenance, firmware updates, and support are included.

Which EV makes and models are compatible?

All Stackly stations use CCS2, CHAdeMO, and Type-2 connectors and comply with ISO 15118. They are compatible with all EVs sold in India including Tata, MG, Ather, Ola, BMW, Mercedes, and more.

What happens if a charger faults?

Our monitoring system detects faults in real time and alerts our NOC. Remote resolution is attempted first. If on-site work is needed, a certified engineer is dispatched within 4 hours under our SLA.

Can I integrate Stackly with my existing building management system?

Yes. Stackly's REST API and OCPP 2.0.1 integration layer connects with leading BMS platforms, Modbus systems, and SAP Energy Management modules. Full API documentation available on request.

Do you offer solar + storage integration?

Absolutely. Stackly's V2G-ready chargers integrate seamlessly with rooftop solar PV systems and battery storage units. We can handle the full design, including net-metering applications with the DISCOM.
